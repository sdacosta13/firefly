Welcome to Firefly

Light Up Manchester

This is Firefly, a group project from 7 first year University of Manchester Computer Science students, group Y1.
This website aims to give an immersive experience for exploring a new city or university, initially targeted at the University of Manchester.
We know how difficult moving somewhere new can be, and hopefully Firefly provides a fun way to learn about the area around you, gaining an insight into the most exciting things to do, the history of the area and more.
Firefly is even more enjoyable with friends, and you can share the locations you have found with friends to learn even more.
We hope you enjoy!

Features

Firefly uses your location data and Google Maps API to help you find new buildings.
Your location is represented by a blue icon, with the red icons being the areas still left to find.
But it won’t be that easy! A dark fog covers the map to make it a little bit more difficult.
Finding a location earns you points based upon the difficulty of each location, and you can earn badges by finding more locations.
You can view your progress from the menu bar, showing your points and badges. Brief descriptions of each location you have found can be seen on the progress page.
If you’re struggling, pictures of different buildings can be seen on the progress page can help.
Friends can share the locations they have found with you, and you can share locations with friends.
